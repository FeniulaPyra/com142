Human Resource Employee

Position Title: Human Resources Generalist

Location: Ethitech Offices, Rochester NY

Salary: $75,000/year

Company Background

Ethitech was founded to fulfill the need for ethics in technology consulting. At Ethitech, we value diverse employee backgrounds, as it ensures we can deliver creative ideas to our associates. Ethitech provides consulting for the ethics of technology solutions to help alleviate future ethical issues in the technology industry.

Job Description

At Ethitech, Human Resources starts with the people and their values. We’re looking for a creative generalist to join our thriving HR department. The ideal candidate will have a willingness to learn and grow on the job. Here at Ethitech, we believe anyone should have a shot at the job. We rely on the people to learn and grow. We expect them to have the ability to collaborate with a team of thinkers and doers. They will be expected to deal with hiring and interviewing staff, administering pay, benefits, and leave, and enforcing company policies and practices.

Responsibilities

  • Oversee employee performance and collaborate with management to evaluate underperforming employees
  • Ensure employee compliance with all company policies and state/federal laws and regulations
  • Establish relationships with all employees in a professional, trustworthy, and open manner
  • Create a welcoming environment for all employees
  • Encourage employee communication with HR
  • Maintain employee records and update as needed
  • Update company policies and benefits package
  • Facilitate communication between management/department heads and employees
  • Educate employees on company benefits and rewards, including promotion and education opportunities
  • Complete payroll paperwork
  • Assist management with corrective action as needed
  • Assist management and department heads with conflict resolution and accidents

Skills and Qualifications

  • Preferred Associate’s in human resources management
  • Preferred 3 years HR experience
  • PHR certified
  • Intermediate experience with Office
  • Strong interpersonal relationship skills
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Continual Learning

Benefits

  • Health and dental insurance
  • Disability Insurance
  • 401k
  • Family Leave
