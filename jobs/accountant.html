Accountant

Position Title: Accountant

Location: Ethitech Offices, Rochester NY

Salary: $92,000/year

Company Background

Ethitech was founded to fulfill the need for ethics in technology consulting. At Ethitech, we value diverse employee backgrounds, as it ensures we can deliver creative ideas to our associates. Ethitech provides consulting for the ethics of technology solutions to help alleviate future ethical issues in the technology industry.

Job Description

At Ethitech, accounting starts with money, and we’re looking for creative accountants to join our ever-important accounting team. The ideal candidates for this position are quick on the uptake and discreet with the financial reports they handle. Ethitech will rely on the accountant's ability to analyze company expenses and present financial reports to the Chief Financial Officer, as well as presenting detailed solutions on how to minimize company expenses. They will be expected to handle sensitive financial documents, and to study company finances to come up with creative solutions on how to maximize profits.

Responsibilities

  • Support the Chief Financial Officer in managing company finances
  • Write financial reports on profits and losses (P&L)
  • Make recommendations to CFO on how to minimize losses
  • Analyze expenses being incurred by employees on the job (including business trips) and seek ways to reduce company expenses.
  • Study federal, state, and local laws to ensure compliance with financial codes and regulations.
  • Write and report cheques
  • Keeping financial reports and records confidential

Skills and Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting or finance or other related field
  • Minimum 1 years of experience in accounting/finance
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to analyze data and write written reports
  • Preferred Experience in communicating with the IRS
  • Continual Learning

  • Telecommuting and work from home options
  • Pay raises
  • Breaks and flexible Schedules
  • College debt Assistance
