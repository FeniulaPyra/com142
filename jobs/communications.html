Communications Coordinator

Position Title: Communications Employee

Location: Ethitech Offices, Rochester NY

Salary: $60,000/year

Company Background

Ethitech was founded to fulfill the need for ethics in technology consulting. At Ethitech, we value diverse employee backgrounds, as it ensures we can deliver creative ideas to our associates. Ethitech provides consulting for the ethics of technology solutions to help alleviate future ethical issues in the technology industry.

Job Description

Ethitech’s Communications Coordinators will be responsible for maintaining a large share of our public image. Tasks will mainly concern the management of Ethitech’s social media presence. Social media management will include: creating and writing posts, sourcing and editing images, or coming up with ideas to boost customer/consumer engagement. Ethitech is currently interested in maintaining image on LinkedIn, Twitter, and FaceBook platforms. In order to communicate effectively, use of Adobe Suite or Microsoft Office applications may be required.

Responsibilities

  • Be able to plan, create, and publish engaging social media content
  • Be able to understand and apply modern project management standards
  • Encourage employee and customer engagement
  • Strive to solidify brand image within the company, and effectively translate that image to company social media.

Skills and Qualifications

  • Familiarity with LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook
  • Intermediate skills in Adobe Suites and Microsoft Office applications
  • At least an Associate’s (2-year) or Bachelor’s (4-year) Degree in Communications
  • At least 1 year of experience in communications

Preferred Skills and Qualifications

  • Experience with social media management
  • Experience with press release

Benefits

  • Health and dental insurance
  • Disability Insurance
  • 401k
  • Family Leave
