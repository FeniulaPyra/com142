About Ethitech

Welcome to Ethitech! We are a technology and ethics consulting company of about 50 people. We provide consulting on the ethics of technology solutions to various technology and technology-adjacent companies in order to curb current and future ethical issues in the technology industry.

Our ethics and technology consulting is a blend of tech solutions and humanity solutions. Our board of directors have a variety of skills and knowledge in many different fields, ensuring a vast range of solutions for our clients. With backgrounds in engineering, game design, and computer information sciences, Ethitech helps eliminate the uncertainties that come with modern technology and innovations.
